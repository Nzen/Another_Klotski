Klotski

Update your browser to the latest version to see what I've drawn on the canvas.

You can move the cursor around the board with IJKL keys. WASD keys will drag that shape under the cursor, provided there's room. Moving the red block to the diagonally hatched area solves the puzzle. I'm glad of this opportunity to learn OO javascript, which is pretty flexible & distinct from class based OO.
